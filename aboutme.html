About Me

My Name is Sidney LaMontagne. I am 19 years old and live in Bangor, Maine Bangor, Maine.

I grew up in a small town with my mom and grandparents, in Southern Maine called West Baldwin near Sebago Lake. My mom married my step dad and we moved to Hudson, Maine From there on we have lived in Bangor, and have lived here since 2006.

One of my passions through life has been film. I have always loved movies since I could remember. The summer before my Freshman year of high school I started to get more into film, I attended a summer camp in Portland called New England Film Academy. It was a two week camp that we created a ten minute script, filmed and edited in a short amount of time.

Throughout high school I always thought I had wanted to pursue film and go to college for film and continue that as a career. During the middle of my Junior Year, I started working at Chick-fil-A. Midway through my Senior Year I really enjoyed working and realized that I wanted to work for Chick-fil-A more and decided to start pursuing different opportunities within the company.

So now here we are in 2019. I am a Business Major at the University of Maine and am pursuing my Bachelors in Business Management with a Minor in Political Science.

I am excited to continue my journey through work and school to see what I am able to succeed in throughout my career.
